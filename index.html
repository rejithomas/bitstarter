What is UnCluttr?

Uncluttr is dedicated to providing a simple seamless social reading and engagement experience. It is beautifully designed, customizable and completely Open Source. You can link in your feeds from Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn and read information that is relevant to you among the clutter of ads, game requests and cat memes. You can carry your experience on your iPhone, Android and Windows Devices sharing it with the world.

Simple Feed Management

UnCluttr does something ridiculously simple when it comes to your content. It lets you browse and preview you all your news feeds within a single view, just like you're already used to with your email. Save time, save energy, browse and manage your social reading experiences without over-click syndrome.

Ready for Sharing and Search

UnCluttr comes ready to connect to your social media services right out of the box. Connect and publish to your Twitter and Facebook accounts, rank properly in search engines, and much more. Moderate and search the feed thats most important to you.

FAQ

Will UnCluttr be mobile friendly?
Inspiration can strike anywhere, not just when you're sitting behind a computer. So, UnCluttr works where you work. It is a fully responsive web application that works on all devices. Not just resizing images and manipulating display. You can access your news feeds, especially stories that are relevant to you and promote them.
Is UnCluttr made for profit?
UnCluttr is a non-profit project. If successfully funded, it will be legally registered as a not-for-profit organisation. Why should you care? It means that no one owns shares of monetary value in the project. We're building UnCluttr because we want online publishing to be better. We want to make decisions focused on that goal, not on building a startup that we can sell to Facebook for $1billion. Being a non-profit means that any money UnCluttr makes can only ever be used to re-invest in making it better. Not making shareholders rich.
